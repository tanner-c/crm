# Game Store CRM - User Guide

Welcome to the Game Store CRM user guide! This manual is designed to help store employees and managers handle everyday store operations like managing customers, updating game inventory, recording sales, and viewing store performance reports.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Accessing the System
1. Open the application in your browser.
2. You will be greeted by the **Sign In** screen.
3. Enter your store email and password, then click **Log In**.

> [!NOTE]
> Your access permissions depend on your user role:
> - **Employee (User)**: Can manage customers, record sales, and browse inventory.
> - **Manager / Admin**: Can do everything an employee does, plus adjust game stock levels, edit prices, create new users, and view financial reports.

---

## 2. Managing Customers

Keeping track of your customer base helps tailor promotions and manage loyalty benefits.

### Adding a New Customer
1. Go to the **Customers** section from the navigation bar.
2. Click the **Add Customer** button.
3. Fill in the customer's name, email, and phone number.
4. Choose their **Loyalty Tier** (Standard, Gold, or Platinum).
5. Click **Save**.

### Tracking Loyalty Tiers
- **Standard**: The default tier for new sign-ups.
- **Gold**: Awarded to frequent shoppers; eligible for special discounts.
- **Platinum**: Awarded to VIP shoppers; eligible for the highest store discounts.
- **Total Spent**: The system automatically adds up a customer's total spending history every time a sale is completed.

---

## 3. Game Inventory (Stock Management)

This module helps you keep track of what games you have in stock, their retail prices, and cover art.

### Searching and Adding Games Automatically
To save time typing, you can search an online game database:
1. Go to the **Inventory** section.
2. Click **Add Game** -> select **Search Database**.
3. Type the name of the game and press enter.
4. Locate the correct version of the game (e.g., matching the platform like PlayStation 5 or Nintendo Switch).
5. Click **Import**. The game's title, platform, genre, description, and cover art will fill in automatically!
6. Enter your retail **Price** and current **Stock Level**, then click **Save**.

### Manually Adding a Game
If a game is not found in the online database:
1. Click **Add Game** -> select **Add Manually**.
2. Type in the game's title, select its platform, genre, and type a description.
3. Enter the retail price and how many copies you currently have in stock.
4. Click **Save**.

---

## 4. Registering a Sale

Use this module when a customer is ready to buy games.

### Recording a Transaction
1. Go to the **Sales** section.
2. Click **New Transaction**.
3. **Select the Customer**: Search by their name or select them from the dropdown list.
4. **Add Games**: Search for the games they are buying and specify the quantity of each.
5. **Set the Status**:
   - **Pending**: Use this if the customer is reserving a copy to buy later.
   - **Completed**: Use this when payment is received and the customer leaves with the games.
6. Click **Finalize Sale**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> - Setting a transaction to **Completed** automatically decreases the stock level of those games in your inventory.
> - If you change a transaction's status to **Cancelled** or delete it, the system automatically returns those copies back to your inventory stock.

---

## 5. Reports (For Managers & Admins)

Managers and Admins can view performance dashboards to see how the store is doing.

### Types of Reports Available
- **Sales Summary**: Shows your total store sales, total revenue, average transaction value, and how many items have been sold.
- **Top Selling Games**: Displays a list of your most popular games by sales quantity and revenue. Use this to determine which titles to re-order.
- **Revenue by Customer**: Lists your top-spending customers so you can reward them.
- **Employee Performance**: Shows sales tallies and revenue generated by each staff member.
